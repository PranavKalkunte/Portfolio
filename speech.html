Confidence

(Skill 2)

Presenter is completely confident in his/her presentation.  Presenter exhibits full knowledge of subject-matter and is superior in explanation of subject-matter to the audience. Presenter commands respect and credibility from the audience.

Presenter is confident in his/her presentation.  Presenter exhibits sufficient knowledge of subject-matter and is competent in the explanation of subject-matter to the audience. Presenter is credible.

Presenter is somewhat confident in his/her presentation.  Presenter exhibits limited knowledge of subject-matter and is somewhat competent in the explanation of subject-matter to the audience. Presenter lacks credibility.

Presenter is not confident in his/her presentation.  Presenter exhibits very little  knowledge of subject-matter and is incompetent in the explanation of subject-matter to the audience. Presenter has no credibility.
