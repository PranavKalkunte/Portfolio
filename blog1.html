Introductory Speech Focused

"Who am I?" - the quintessential question. Surely I know myself, I've spent my entire life with me. Yet what I struggled with was forming it into words. A complex characterization on many years, how do I summarize myself into a short speech of three to five minutes? First impressions last forever goes the proverb - if only I remembered my own. Moreover, once I set upon the task of formalizing my character onto paper, a feel of dread overcomes me. I fear that I would be nothing more than a few words and an emptiness for the rest of the page. Two opposing contradictions doubled down on this anxiety. On one hand, I am unable to present who I am in such a literal form yet I worry that there might not be much to present. Do I even really want to know who I am?
